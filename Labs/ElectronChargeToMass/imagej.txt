ImageJ and e/m data

1. Load image into ImageJ

2. Scale image step 1: Draw a line covering a known distance

3. Scale image step 2: Image->Scale

4. On the tool bar, right click on "point tool" to make it into "multipoint" tool.

5. Click along electron beam path. Small yellow numbered point/placeholders should appear.

6. Edit->Selection->Select None (if needed to clear/start over)

7. File->Save As...->XY Coordinates
